Current: [`src/adapters/fs.rs#L1239`](/root/new-ralph-burning/ralph-burning-rewrite/src/adapters/fs.rs#L1239) reads the lock through an fd, compares inode metadata at [`src/adapters/fs.rs#L1275`](/root/new-ralph-burning/ralph-burning-rewrite/src/adapters/fs.rs#L1275), then drops the fd and unlinks by pathname at [`src/adapters/fs.rs#L1294`](/root/new-ralph-burning/ralph-burning-rewrite/src/adapters/fs.rs#L1294). That risk propagates to CLI guard cleanup at [`src/contexts/automation_runtime/cli_writer_lease.rs#L160`](/root/new-ralph-burning/ralph-burning-rewrite/src/contexts/automation_runtime/cli_writer_lease.rs#L160) and stale CLI reconcile at [`src/contexts/automation_runtime/lease_service.rs#L478`](/root/new-ralph-burning/ralph-burning-rewrite/src/contexts/automation_runtime/lease_service.rs#L478).
